About Blitz

Driven by passion, Blitz is a global leader in digital interactive entertainment.

Headquartered in Redwood City in Northern California, Blitz develops and delivers games, content, and online services for Internet-connected consoles, mobile devices, and personal computers. Through its cutting-edge games, innovative services, and powerful technologies, Blitz brings worlds with infinite possibilities to nearly 600 million active players and fans around the globe. Blitz is recognized for a portfolio of critically acclaimed, high-quality brands such as Blitz SPORTS™ FIFA, Battlefield™, Apex Legends™, The Sims™, Madden NFL, Need for Speed™, Titanfall™, and F1®.

Our People

Everything Blitz does is designed to inspire the world to play, and Blitz believes it's at its best when listening, learning, and empowering each other. Blitz celebrates openness and curiosity and is committed to making a positive impact in the world. Blitz hires creative, collaborative, inclusive people with diverse backgrounds who enrich its culture and challenge Blitz to be better people and better at what it does. Blitz nurtures inclusive leaders who foster a belonging culture – a place where everyone can thrive, fulfill their potential, and make the best games.

Our Practices

Blitz believes in the power of positive play and that being part of a gaming community should be a fun, fair, and safe experience for all. Blitz is equally committed to building a workplace that spurs creativity and innovation and is as diverse as the communities it serves. As a leading global entertainment business, Blitz wants to inspire through its actions and create a positive impact in the world. This isn’t a destination but an ongoing journey of transparency and continual progress.
